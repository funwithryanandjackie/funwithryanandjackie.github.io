No children are allowed to swim without supervision. No responsibility for their safety can be accepted by us or Pat Busch.


Accomodation

We have limited accommodation at the venue and so unfortunately not everyone will fit, it will be on a first come first served basis. We may need people to share cottages/rooms so that we can fit as many people as possible.

Standard Cottages – R400 pp for the weekend (26th & 27th)
Farmhouse – R250 pp for the weekend (26th & 27th)
Camping – R100 pppn (please organise with Pat Busch directly)

Check in from 2pm on Friday ~ Check out by 10am Sunday

If you would like to arrive a day or 2 earlier or stay a couple of days after the wedding please organize directly with Pat Busch. We will be staying from Thursday to Monday.

If you would like to stay at a different location we can recommend some places in the area.

Food

Friday night – bring and braai for anyone who’s there that night, we’ll provide some salads and rolls, please bring anything else you want to munch on.
Saturday – we’ll also be providing brunch on the day of the wedding. All the food on Saturday will be vegetarian and there'll be vegan options too.

Pat Busch offer a cook your own breakfast basket for R110 per person which they deliver to you. It includes health bread, muesli, milk, cheese, local bacon, eggs, fruit juice, ground coffee for use in the plungers, rusks, fresh homemade fruit salad, yoghurt and jams.

Please order this with Pat Busch directly a few days before the wedding so that they can stock up on the fresh things.

Drinks

At the reception there will be an open bar with a limited tab stocked with beer, wine and soft drinks. Once that has run out it will turn into a cash bar.

For the rest of the weekend you are on your own. Robertson is close by so you can always pop to the shops to restock on anything you need.
